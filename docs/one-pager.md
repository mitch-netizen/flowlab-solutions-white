# FlowLab Solutions
## The operating system for your field service business.

---

### The problem

You're running a field service business on your own. You quote on WhatsApp, invoice on Xero when you remember, track customers in a spreadsheet, and follow up jobs when you get a minute. Nothing is connected. Leads slip through. Invoices go out late. You don't know what's actually on tomorrow until you check three different apps.

---

### What FlowLab does

FlowLab is a white-label platform that gives sole operators and small field service teams a single, branded hub for every part of the job — from first enquiry to paid invoice.

It runs as five distinct apps, all connected:

| App | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| **Overview** | Your morning brief — jobs on today, outstanding tasks, key numbers |
| **CRM** | Every customer in one place. Enquiries, job history, communications, feedback |
| **Jobs** | Swim-lane board: Quoted → Scheduled → In Progress → Complete → Invoiced → Paid |
| **Revenue** | Xero-backed invoices. Every invoice linked to a job and a customer |
| **Setup** | Connect Xero, configure automations, manage your branding |

Everything is linked. A customer has one Xero Contact. A job has one invoice. An invoice has one Xero record. Nothing inferred, nothing guessed.

---

### Key integrations

**Xero** — Invoicing source of truth. When you create an invoice in FlowLab it goes to Xero immediately as AUTHORISED. Your Xero account stays clean and complete.

**DocuSeal** — Service agreements. Quote accepted → agreement generated and sent for signature automatically. Stored against the job.

**Make.com** — Automation blueprints. Feedback requests, Google review prompts, rebook reminders — all running without you touching them.
